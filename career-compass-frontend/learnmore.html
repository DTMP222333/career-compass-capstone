Master Numbers

If any of your numbers is 11, 22, or 33, you have a Master Number. These numbers carry higher potential and combine the strengths of their base numbers (2, 4, or 6) with enhanced intuition, leadership, or compassion.

Why Numerology?


Numerology has been used for centuries to understand patterns in personality, motivation, and life direction. For our Career Compass project, numerology helps translate your natural strengths into career-aligned insights.

Each number represents a specific part of “you”:

• Life Path → your natural direction (35%)

• Expression → how you operate & communicate (25%)

• Personality → how others see you (20%)

• Soul Urge → your inner motivation (10%)

• Birth Number → your innate gift (10%)


Together, these form a complete picture of your traits — allowing the system to suggest careers that match your strengths.


How to Use This Section


Users are instructed to:

• Find their five numbers

• Read the description + statement for each number

• Copy the five statements

• Combine them into a personal narrative such as:

“As a Life Path 7, I'm naturally analytical and introspective. With an Expression 5, I communicate with adaptability and versatility. My Personality 8 gives me a strong presence and ambition. My Soul Urge 6 motivates me to support and care for others. And my Birth Number 1 gives me natural leadership instincts.”
This gives the user a complete and personalized career identity paragraph.
